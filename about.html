About Goldleaf

  • Goldleafis a cozy breakfast and lunch café located in the heart of Portland, Maine. We’re known for our warm atmosphere, friendly service, and delicious food made from scratch using locally sourced ingredients.
  • Whether you're craving a hearty breakfast sandwich, a fresh pastry, or just a perfect cup of coffee, we’ve got something for everyone. Our menu combines comfort food classics with a modern twist.
  • Founded by passionate food lovers, Goldleaf has become a favorite spot for locals and visitors alike. We’re proud to support our community and create a space where people can relax, connect, and enjoy great meals.
  • At Goldleaf, every dish is made with love — and every guest is treated like family.
